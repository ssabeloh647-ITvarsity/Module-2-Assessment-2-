Shoprite

The Shoprite Group of Companies, is Africa's largest food retailer. It operates more than 2,892 stores in 14 countries across Africa. The company's headquarters are in Brackenfell in the Western Cape province of South Africa. The Group's primary business is food retailing to consumers of all income levels. Freshmark is South Africa's largest fruit and vegetable distributor. They distribute produce to over 440 Shoprite Group stores as well as other retail outlets.Our comprehensive range of food, household products, furniture, pharmaceuticals, financial services, cellular services and ancillary services has resulted in our stores becoming one-stop shopping destinations where shoppers can enjoy the lowest prices, value for money and convenience.
